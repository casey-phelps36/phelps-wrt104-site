Why I Chose This?

I chose this topic because I am very passionate about rock climbing, and I wanted to explore the gyms in my area. The inspiration for this project was my Europe trip this past summer. Every place we went to I would find the closest gym and go try it out, and I had so much fun seeing new gyms and learning

My Climbing Background

I have been climbing since 2021. During the school year I try to climb about twice a week, but during the summer I climb about four times a week. The highest grade I have been able to climb is a v6, and I can consitently get v5s.
